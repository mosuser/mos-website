How will MOS come to the future?

In the recent currency shocks, mainstream currencies plummeted overnight, and the secondary market was raging, and the quantum hegemony that threatened the security of Bitcoin private keys was rampant. The highly-recognized “rich second-generation” exchange Bakkt went online after trading. The volume was so bad. The news caused the market to panic, whether the encrypted digital currency has reached the bear market cycle? Can there be strength projects to turn the tide?

In the downturn of the market, the innovative project MOS Moss broke through the turmoil and sprung up. It took the DAO 2.0 concept and the innovative economic model to seize the opportunity. It was regarded by the industry as the next new ecological market, and the MOS project became the model. Inciting capital leverage?

MOS project breaks homogenization and eliminates the sudden rise and fall of tokens.

At present, the currency project is highly homogenized and lacks a healthy mode of operation. Many project coins cannot escape the short-lived fate of the survival cycle. Usually, after two or three price fluctuations, there will be long-term sideways, and then it will start to fall. The project side will not be able to return to the sky at this time, because in the continuous decline of the currency price, investors will adjust their expectations, and most of them only seek back. It will not be bargain-hunt. At this time, as long as the project side pulls the plate, the plate will appear. The biggest village will turn to the amaranth by the project side, and the collective trampling panic will even lead to the zero price of the currency. The blockchain project has long been stuck in this infinite loop, with serious homogenization and no healthy model.

The MOS project is a decentralized self-organizing financial services operating system based on DAO based on a great technological innovation called DAO2.0. The MOS project uses the DAO 2.0 concept and a highly competitive and differentiated economic model to break the homogenization of the currency project. It advocates the community consensus to determine the value of the token, avoiding the centralization operation and causing the currency price to plummet. Become a dark horse in the industry!

The MOS project has designed a new organizational structure (ie, commissioned underwriting) that includes token issuers, underwriting nodes, sub-underwriting nodes, and other roles. These roles are interrelated and mutually motivated by distributed rules, effectively avoiding tradition. The market leader of the primary market project has purified the market environment for the possibility of evil in the interests of the people.

 

In the DAO 2.0 architecture of the MOS project, in order to solve the decentralization problem in the impossible triangle, a random mechanism based on VRF (Verifiable Random Function) is adopted, that is, each time a topic, project, and voting are decided. In the process of adding certain random factors, avoiding the reliance on specific members and the possibility of stakeholders paying bribes to known members, truly achieve “decentralization.”

The development of a decentralized organization is inseparable from the support of the nodes, which requires corresponding rewards for their contributions. In the past DAO 1.0 project, it relied only on a single mechanism such as workload (PoW), equity (PoS), etc., which limited the flexibility of various applications derived from the organization. Therefore, in the MOS project, a consensus mechanism called PoFC (Proof of Finance Contribution) was innovatively adopted. For the first time, two different types of incentives were combined in the form of weights: this greatly improved Flexibility and scalability of applications derived from MOS projects.

Members and investors, in assessing the development prospects of each project, used to rely on their own experience, resulting in a lot of uncertainty and risk. To this end, the MOS project uses the Endogenous Growth Theory (EGT) established by 2018 Nobel Prize winner in economics, Paul Roemer, to analyze the future trends and growth rates of each project. The parameters used include the number of tokens of the project, the number of nodes, and the development investment. After the EGT-based empirical formula is transformed, the quantitative growth curve of each project is obtained, which greatly reduces the members and investors when selecting the project. Blindness.

The MOS project, which is mainly for various financial applications, provides a set of “DAF” (Decentralized Autonomous Finance) protocol groups for various application projects, and has developed the corresponding MOSDAO APP. In this way, project parties can easily use these protocols and infrastructure to develop application scenarios and services that they and their members need.

MOS project will rely on careful architecture design, advanced technology, careful logic setting, strong intelligent contract support and unique cross-chain and sidechain technology to meet the needs of various financial industry applications and derivatives. The first choice for the service industry.

In terms of the single mechanism relative to other projects, the MOS project has created a healthy and benign eco-coin loop, and the project tokens will not go to an uncontrollable abyss.

A new benchmark for digital currency value – MOS coins

1.The huge advantage of the entrusted underwriting mechanism benefited by the three parties

Before the traditional blockchain project tokens are purchased in the secondary market, they will be pre-sold in the primary market for angel round financing. MOS coins are sold in the world’s first DAO tokens, and follow the MOS project to provide a commissioned underwriting mechanism to effectively reward project contributors under the interests of the three parties and provide reasonable solutions for token sales.

The token issuer issues tokens at the MOSDAO Morse Council, so there is no need to worry about the sales of the project tokens. The tokens are sold by the highly influential core underwriting nodes that have been evaluated, and the sub-underwriting nodes are professionally subject to the core underwriting nodes. With the help of investment ability, there is no need to worry about the project side running in the project selection, and the loss of the lock and other factors will cause huge property losses. From this point of view, the issuer of each token only needs to focus on the issue of token value enhancement, so that the project can grow rapidly; the core underwriting node not only expands its brand influence in the process of selling tokens, You can also enjoy the rewards of the mechanism; the sub-underwriting node can quickly identify high-quality projects, and greatly reduce the investment risk and achieve the return on investment!

2.MOS coins use the mechanism of the release

At present, many project tokens will be partially locked, which is undoubtedly a disguised scam, because most of the early period of token issuance is steadily rising, and even the previous currency price is likely to reach the highest point of the currency in the whole period. Locking the warehouse will bring potential losses to investors.

MOS coins will be opened on the exchange of DAO on the MODSAO Moss parliament. At the same time, the principal and interest will not lock the position, and the token trading will be unimpeded and the interests of investors will be guaranteed.

3.MOS tokens use the hierarchy and thousands of deflationary rising mechanism

Most of the projects in the industry have short life cycles, and investors are accustomed to the mentality of holding short-term investments. When the price of the currency falls, the collective panic is no matter how the project pulls, and the currency circle is only strong. MOS Moss pioneered “hierarchy”

And the thousand-fold deflationary rise mechanism” and the rising mechanism is constrained by smart contracts, others can not tamper with, and truly maintain the long-term stable upward trend of the project MOS tokens.

The initial issue price of MOS: MOS token and USDT initial exchange ratio is 10000USDT/14000MOS

Currency price appreciation rules: MOS adopts the “hierarchy” growth and issuance mechanism, and each 75 million tokens is set to one stage, and the initial DAO issuance pool of 75 million tokens is the first order of issuance. In the first stage, 7.5 million tokens were set as one floor. For each layer sold, the price of coins increased by 0.00143USDT. From the second stage, the number of tokens set on each floor is ten times deflated, and the value of each floor price change is unchanged at 0.00143USDT. For example: After the sales of 75 million tokens in the initial release pool of DAO in the first stage, each 7500 tokens (7.5 million/10) is set to one floor, and the price per layer increases by 0.00143USDT. The post-release phase rules are analogous.
