MOS Asia Pacific node representative visits the ambassador of the Federated States of Micronesia in China

You are here:
  1. Home
  2. NEWS
  3. MOS Asia Pacific node representative…

On the afternoon of September 28th, Mr. Thomas, the representative of the MOS Asia Pacific node, visited the Embassy of the Federated States of Micronesia (hereinafter referred to as the “Federal Union”) in Beijing. Ambassador Carlson D. Apis, Ambassador of the Federal Embassy in China, met with Mr. Thomas and his party. The two sides held talks on promoting MOS and Micronesia blockchain financial infrastructure.

At the symposium, Lord Carl Appis expressed his gratitude to the MOS team for providing the opportunity to provide blockchain technical training to the students of the Federal Republic. He said: “I have been paying attention to the blockchain field for 2 years. Blockchain technology is an Internet-based distributed ledger technology with decentralization, high security, low credit cost, inability to tamper with and transparency. It provides a new credit creation mechanism and has broad application prospects in the financial field. The secret federation attaches great importance to blockchain technology. We believe that the blockchain will not even have a subversive effect on the financial industry. Bring a huge impact.”

His Excellency Carl Appis gave a positive assessment of MOS’s contribution to the financial industry. The Federal Embassy in China will fully support the MOS project and cooperate with Micronesia and Micronesia. Lord Carl Apis pointed out that Micronesia College is the only university in the United States, shouldering the heavy responsibility of cultivating national talents and supporting the development of national industries. MOS is the builder of global financial infrastructure. And sincerely invite the MOS team to visit Micronesia.

Mr. Thomas, the representative of the MOS Asia Pacific node, thanked Carl Apis for his active matchmaking and promoted the cooperation between MOS and Micronesia. He said that MOS is a new generation of blockchain financial infrastructure based on DAO2.0. It is recommended that both parties can cooperate as soon as possible, and sincerely invites Lord Carl Apis and the President of Micronesia to visit MOS to learn about The situation of disciplines and industrial development is conducive to enhancing mutual understanding and solidifying the foundation of cooperation.
