Technological innovation

The super-application MOSDAO Morse Council under the MOS project first realized the breakthrough of the MOS project feature technology.

Because the MOS project designed a new organizational structure (ie, commissioned underwriting) that includes token issuers, underwriting nodes, sub-underwriting nodes, and other roles, these roles are interrelated and mutually motivated by distributed rules, effectively avoiding The traditional primary market project side platform people have purified the market environment for the possibility of evil.

In the DAO 2.0 architecture of the MOS project, in order to solve the decentralization problem in the impossible triangle, a random mechanism based on VRF (Verifiable Random Function) is adopted, that is, each time a topic, project, and voting are decided. In the process of adding certain random factors, avoiding the reliance on specific members and the possibility of stakeholders paying bribes to known members, truly achieve “decentralization.”

The development of a decentralized organization is inseparable from the support of the nodes, which requires corresponding rewards for their contributions. In the past DAO 1.0 project, it relied only on a single mechanism such as workload (PoW), equity (PoS), etc., which limited the flexibility of various applications derived from the organization. Therefore, in the MOS project, Chuangw Xindi adopted a consensus mechanism called Poof (Proof of Finance Contribution), which for the first time combines two different types of incentives in the form of weights: this greatly improves The flexibility and expandability of the applications derived from the MOS project.

Members and investors, in assessing the development prospects of each project, used to rely on their own experience, resulting in a lot of uncertainty and risk. To this end, the MOS project uses the Endogenous Growth Theory (EGT) established by Nobel Prize-winning economist Paul Romer to analyze the future trends and growth rates of each project. The parameters used include the number of tokens of the project, the number of nodes, and the development investment. After the EGT-based empirical formula is transformed, the quantitative growth curve of each project is obtained, which greatly reduces the members and investors when selecting the project. Blindness.

In this way, project parties can easily use these protocols and infrastructure to develop application scenarios and services that they and their members need.

MOS project will rely on careful architecture design, advanced technology, careful logic setting, strong intelligent contract support and unique cross-chain and sidechain technology to meet the needs of various financial industry applications and derivatives. Service industry preferred
